Isaac, M., et al. (2000). "6-bicyclopiperazinyl-1-arylsulfonylindoles and 6-bicyclopiperidinyl-1-arylsulfonylindoles derivatives as novel, potent, and selective 5-HT6 receptor antagonists." Bioorg Med Chem Lett 10(15): 1719-1721.

	A novel series of 6-bicyclopiperazinyl-1-arylsulfonylindoles and 6-bicyclopiperidinyl-1-arylsulfonylindoles derivatives was synthesized and found to be potent and selective 5-HT6 receptor antagonists. (C) 2000 Elsevier Science Ltd. All rights reserved.
