Becker, D. P., et al. (2004). "Azaadamantane benzamide 5-HT4 agonists: gastrointestinal prokinetic SC-54750." Bioorganic & Medicinal Chemistry Letters 14(22): 5509-5512.

	Azaadamantanone 1 was converted to a series of aminoazaadamantane benzamides 9a-d, which were profiled for serotonin receptor activity. Aminomethylazaadamantane SC-54750 is a potent 5-HT4 agonist and 5-HT3 antagonist with in vivo efficacy in gastroparesis models and also inhibits cisplatin-induced emesis. (C) 2004 Elsevier Ltd.
