Wu, Y. J., et al. (2003). "Identification of a potent and selective 5-HT6 antagonist: One-step synthesis of (E)-3-(benzenesulfonyl)-2-(methylsulfanyl)pyrido 1,2-a pyrimidin-4-ylide namine from 2-(benzenesulfonyl)-3,3-bis(methylsulfanyl)acrylonitrile." J Med Chem 46(23): 4834-4837.

	(E)-3-(Benzenesulfonyl)-2-(methylsulfanyl)pyrido[1,2-alpha]pyrimidin-4-ylidenamine (7) was found to be a potent and selective 5-HT6 antagonist. A one-step synthesis of this compound is described.
